How to make Peanut Butter Pie

With just a few simple ingredients and a couple of hours to spare, you can recreate the magic of this beloved dessert right in your own kitchen. This recipe for peanut butter pie is not only easy to follow but also promises to deliver the same decadent satisfaction as your favorite café treat. So, if you're ready to embark on a culinary adventure that'll delight your taste buds and warm your soul, gather your ingredients and let's get started on this journey.

Ingredients

  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
  • ½ cup creamy peanut butter
  • ½ cup confectioners' sugar
  • 1 (16 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed, divided
  • 1 (9 inch) prepared graham cracker crust
  • 15 miniature chocolate-covered peanut butter cups (such as Reese's), unwrapped

Directions

  1. Mix cream cheese, peanut butter, and confectioners' sugar together until smooth.

  2. Fold in 1/2 of the whipped topping.

  3. Spoon the mixture into graham cracker crust.

  4. Spread remaining whipped topping over the peanut butter mixture.

  5. Garnish with peanut butter cups.

  6. Chill for at least 2 hours or overnight.
